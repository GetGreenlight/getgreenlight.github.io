How Greenlight helps

  • Notifies you on your phone or watch whenever an agent needs your input.
  • Smart rules grant an agent just the permissions it needs, and can auto-expire them.
  • The sessions view shows you your active agents, and whether they're working, waiting or idling.

The story

As the creators of Greenlight, we understand the challenge of creating successful software whilst balancing commitments. Coding agents are a great tool, but they demand too much of your time. Time that could be spent designing a new feature, talking to users or generating leads.

We built Greenlight to make it easy to keep your agents working. It runs on your computer, not in the cloud somewhere. All agent chats are privately transmitted to your phone, and never stored. It works with Claude, Codex and other top coding agents so you're not locked in to any one supplier. We started by keeping a single agent unblocked — now, with Autopilot, Greenlight can run the whole pipeline from ticket to merged. Give it a try, so instead of losing time herding agents, you're directing your vision of the future.
